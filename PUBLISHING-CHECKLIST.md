# Pre-publication and AdSense readiness checklist

## Required before launch

- [x] Contact email configured: `mcqenglish16@gmail.com`.
- [x] Public URL, canonical links, `robots.txt`, and `sitemap.xml` configured for `https://mcqenglish.github.io/`.
- [ ] Search all files for `YOUR-` and remove every placeholder.
- [ ] Confirm the site name, owner/contact details, and privacy wording are accurate.
- [ ] Check all menu and footer links on desktop and mobile.
- [ ] Complete at least several quizzes in every category and difficulty.
- [ ] Verify contact email opens correctly.
- [ ] Use a public repository or custom-domain deployment that Google can crawl.

## Content quality

- [ ] Add regular original lessons based on reader needs.
- [ ] Do not publish dozens of near-duplicate keyword pages.
- [ ] Correct ambiguous or inaccurate quiz items.
- [ ] Keep visible publication/update dates honest.
- [ ] Avoid copyrighted answer keys, books, images, and scraped articles.

## AdSense application

- [ ] Applicant is at least 18, or an eligible adult applies.
- [ ] Applicant controls the site and can edit its HTML.
- [ ] Entire site complies with Google Publisher Policies.
- [ ] Site is complete, live, navigable, and has substantial text.
- [ ] AdSense verification code is copied exactly from the account.
- [ ] Privacy policy is updated for cookies, identifiers, partners, and consent.
- [ ] A certified CMP or Google Privacy & messaging is configured where required.
- [ ] `ads.txt` is created only with the exact publisher ID after it is available.

## After ads are enabled

- [ ] Never click your own ads or ask users to click them.
- [ ] Do not place more ads than content.
- [ ] Keep ads separate from quiz answers, navigation, and action buttons.
- [ ] Monitor Search Console, site errors, policy messages, and traffic quality.
