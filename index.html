A simple study loop

Learn, practise, review, repeat

English improves faster when explanation and recall work together. Use this four-step loop for every topic.

Read the exam strategy guide
  1. 1
    Learn one rule

    Read a focused lesson and explain the key idea in your own words.

  2. 2
    Answer without notes

    Take a short test and make a genuine attempt before checking.

  3. 3
    Review the reason

    Study why the correct option works and why your choice failed.

  4. 4
    Return later

    Repeat the topic after a gap so the rule moves into long-term memory.

Frequently asked questions

Before you begin

Is EnglishArena free?

Yes. The lessons and quizzes can be used without creating an account. No payment information is requested.

Are the questions suitable for every exam?

The site teaches general English grammar and vocabulary. Exam boards differ, so use your official syllabus and past papers alongside these materials.

Where is my score stored?

Quiz totals are saved in local browser storage on your device. EnglishArena does not run a user-account database.

Can a question contain an error?

We review content carefully, but errors are possible. Use the contact page to report the question ID and the issue so it can be checked.
