About EnglishArena

Clear English learning without unnecessary barriers

EnglishArena is an independent educational website that combines concise reference lessons with browser-based practice tests.

Our purpose

Many learners can recognise a grammar rule during a lesson but struggle to recall it during an exam or real conversation. EnglishArena connects explanation and retrieval practice: read a focused guide, answer questions without notes, and review the reason behind each answer.

What the site provides

The learning library covers core grammar, vocabulary, spelling, and exam strategy. The quiz tool offers categories, difficulty levels, optional timing, instant scoring, and answer review. It works without registration and stores simple progress totals in the visitor’s own browser.

What the site does not claim

EnglishArena is not an official exam board, school, university, or language-certification provider. The quiz generator creates many combinations from curated source material; it does not claim that every possible combination was written individually. Results are for personal practice and are not formal qualifications.

Continuous improvement

Educational material can contain mistakes or become unclear as usage guidance evolves. We encourage readers to report a specific page, sentence, or question ID. Corrections are reviewed and meaningful changes are recorded during site updates.

Core principles
  • Original explanations
  • Transparent limitations
  • Accessible design
  • Privacy-conscious defaults
  • Correction-friendly publishing
