Privacy policy

How information is handled

This policy describes the current static website. It must be updated before adding analytics, advertising, forms, or other third-party services.

Effective date: 19 July 2026

1. Information stored on your device

The quiz feature uses browser local storage to remember the number of completed tests, the number of answered questions, and the best percentage score. This information remains on your device and is not sent to an EnglishArena account or server. You can remove it with the “Reset local progress” button or by clearing browser site data.

2. Server logs

The hosting provider may automatically process technical information such as an IP address, browser type, requested page, date, time, and security events. EnglishArena does not control the hosting provider’s necessary infrastructure logs. Review the privacy terms of the provider used to publish this site.

3. Contact messages

When you contact the site owner, the information you voluntarily provide may be used to answer your message, investigate a correction, or prevent abuse. Do not send sensitive personal information.

4. Cookies and advertising

The downloadable version of this website does not include analytics or advertising code. If Google AdSense or another advertising service is added, this policy must be revised to explain cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, advertising identifiers, data sharing, consent choices, and links to relevant partner policies. Where legally required, an appropriate consent-management platform must be configured before personalised advertising is served.

5. External links

Pages may link to external resources. Those websites have their own privacy practices, and EnglishArena is not responsible for their content or data handling.

6. Children

The site provides general educational material and is not designed to collect personal profiles from children. No user registration is offered. A parent, guardian, or teacher should supervise a child’s use where appropriate.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated when the site’s technology or legal obligations change. The effective date will be revised when a material update is published.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through the contact page. The site owner must add a verified contact method before public launch.
